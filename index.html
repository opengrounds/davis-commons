open:grounds
a citizen map of shared resources
◎ davis, california

open:grounds is a citizen-built map of common goods in davis, ca. free, shared, and mutual-aid resources that belong to everyone: community gardens, freedges, the food co-op, little free libraries, tool libraries, thrift and reuse stores, bike repair, and more.
